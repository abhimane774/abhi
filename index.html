About Us

Our group is working on a designing of a software which can be used for management of expenses on an individual or a community for the better utilization of money. Our Application helps individual or a group to know their expenses ranging from a day to any year. One can enter their income with the specified date and also update the same. Then he/she can enter their corresponding expenses on the corresponding dates and can get a daily/monthly/yearly report of his expenses and his savings. We are four in group and all worked together for this project.
